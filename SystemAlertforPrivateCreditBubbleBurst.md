#Glossary

Abbreviation	Full Term	Definition	Reference

ACFI	Alternative Credit and Fixed Income	Magnetar Capital's multi-strategy credit platform encompassing structured credit, distressed debt, and liquid credit strategies	(GrowthCap Advisory, 2025)
AUM	Assets Under Management	Total market value of assets that a financial institution or fund manages on behalf of clients; key metric for firm scale	(S&P Global Market Intelligence, 2025b)
BDC	Business Development Company	U.S. publicly-traded investment vehicle regulated under the Investment Company Act of 1940, designed to provide capital to small and mid-sized private companies; offers retail investor access to private credit	(U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission, 2025)
BCRED	Blackstone Private Credit Fund	Blackstone's landmark non-traded BDC offering retail investors access to private credit strategies; reached $82B AUM in 2025	(Private Debt Investor, 2025)
CRE	Commercial Real Estate	Income-generating property assets (office, retail, industrial, multifamily) often financed through private credit structures	(Fitch Ratings, 2025)
CLO	Collateralized Loan Obligation	Structured finance vehicle that pools leveraged loans and issues tranches of debt securities with varying risk/return profiles	(S&P Global Market Intelligence, 2025b)
DPI	Distributed to Paid-In Capital	Private equity/credit performance metric measuring cumulative distributions returned to investors relative to capital contributed	(Preqin, 2025)
DSCR	Debt Service Coverage Ratio	Financial ratio measuring a borrower's cash flow available to service debt obligations; key underwriting metric in private credit	(Moody's, 2026)
EBITDA	Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization	Proxy for operating cash flow; primary metric used to assess borrower capacity and set leverage multiples in private credit underwriting	(Fitch Ratings, 2026)
ESG	Environmental, Social, and Governance	Framework for evaluating sustainability and ethical impact of investments; increasingly integrated into private credit due diligence	(Brookfield Asset Management, 2025)
GP	General Partner	Entity that manages a private fund, makes investment decisions, and typically receives management fees and carried interest	(S&P Global Market Intelligence, 2025b)
IRR	Internal Rate of Return	Discount rate that makes the net present value of all cash flows equal to zero; standard metric for measuring private fund performance	(Preqin, 2025)
LBO	Leveraged Buyout	Acquisition of a company using significant borrowed capital, often financed in part by private credit direct lending	(S&P Global Market Intelligence, 2025b)
LP	Limited Partner	Institutional or accredited investor that commits capital to a private fund but has no role in day-to-day management	(S&P Global Market Intelligence, 2025b)
LTV	Loan-to-Value	Ratio of loan amount to appraised value of collateral; key risk metric in asset-based private credit lending	(Moody's, 2026)
MOIC	Multiple on Invested Capital	Performance metric calculated as total value (realized + unrealized) divided by invested capital; common in private markets reporting	(Preqin, 2025)
NAV	Net Asset Value	Total value of a fund's assets minus liabilities, divided by outstanding shares; critical metric for BDCs and evergreen funds	(U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission, 2025)
NDFI	Non-Depository Financial Institution	Financial entities that do not accept traditional deposits (e.g., private credit funds, hedge funds, BDCs); key counterparty for bank lending exposure	(Forbes, 2026)
PE	Private Equity	Asset class involving direct investment in private companies or buyouts of public companies; often partners with private credit for capital structures	(S&P Global Market Intelligence, 2025b)
PIK	Payment-in-Kind	Loan feature allowing borrowers to pay interest by issuing additional debt rather than cash; "bad PIKs" signal cash flow stress	(Forbes, 2025; Bloomberg News, 2025)
RVPI	Residual Value to Paid-In Capital	Metric measuring unrealized value relative to capital contributed; indicates remaining potential value in a private fund	(Preqin, 2025)
TMT	Technology, Media, and Telecom	Sector classification frequently targeted by private credit; currently facing elevated risk from AI disruption and valuation resets	(Seeking Alpha, 2026)
TVPI	Total Value to Paid-In Capital	Sum of DPI and RVPI; comprehensive metric of fund performance including both realized and unrealized returns	(Preqin, 2025)
XLF	Financial Select Sector SPDR Fund	ETF tracking the financial sector of the S&P 500; used as benchmark for financial sector performance	(Bloomberg News, 2026)
YTD	Year-to-Date	Period from the beginning of the current calendar or fiscal year to the present date; common performance reporting timeframe	(S&P Global Market Intelligence, 2026)
